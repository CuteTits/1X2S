When You Create an Account:

6. GDPR Rights

As a user in the EU, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccuracies
  • Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at contact@1x2s.comarrow_outward.

7. California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Request access to and deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (Note: 1X2S does not sell personal information)
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your rights

To make a CCPA request, email us at contact@1x2s.comarrow_outward with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.

8. Data Retention

We retain account data only as long as your account is active or as needed to comply with legal obligations. IP and locational data are retained temporarily for security monitoring and are not used further.

9. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your personal data and use appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, including:

  • Secure transport of data using HTTPS (TLS encryption)
  • Password hashing and salting using industry-standard algorithms
  • Strict access controls and administrative policies
  • Firewalls and DDoS protection provided via Cloudflare
  • Regular internal monitoring and review of access logs
  • System updates and security patches applied promptly

While no system can be 100% secure, we continually review and update our security practices to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13 (or under 16 where applicable under GDPR). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
