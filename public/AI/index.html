100% Performance, 0% Footprint

Static 2.0, Built on Mistral 7B is a compact, performance-oriented language model designed around MoE (Mixture of Experts) architecture, which allows it to deliver high computational efficiency while remaining lightweight. Its architecture is optimized for applications where speed, responsiveness, and resource efficiency are critical.

To further enhance its capabilities, we fine-tune Mistral using QLoRA (Low-Rank Adaptation). This approach significantly reduces compute requirements during training while maintaining or even improving model performance, making it an environmentally friendly choice without compromising on output quality.

Our models are hosted on AWS servers, and we operate with a strong commitment to sustainability. By leveraging cloud infrastructure with carbon-neutral initiatives, we ensure that the environmental footprint of running advanced AI models is minimized.

Digital doesn't always mean environmentally friendly, but together we work towards net-zero operations.
